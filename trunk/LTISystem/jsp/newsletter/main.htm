MyPlanIQ NewsLetter


June 4th, 2010


MyPlanIQ BiWeekly -- A plain spoken investment newsletter

When was the last time you opened a 401K statement from your provider? Remember the dark days of 2008 and 2009 when your 401K shrank to 201K?

Boomers are starting to utter the 'R' word while striving to adjust to the long term economic trends of the East rising, massive budget deficits and continuing trade imbalances. It is more important than ever to take care of your retirement and financial future. Since we all work hard for our money, why shouldn't our money work hard for us? For example, an extra 6% return for a decade doubles your money.

That's all very nice, but what about the expense, effort and risk of investing? Some turn to financial experts, paying high fees, not having a clue how to invest. A few brave souls search for guidance, only to be disappointed by the inconsistent and unsystematic "advice".

MyPlanIQ was born out of this background and experience. We are in a unique position to discuss and address getting your money to work harder for you. The purpose of this newsletter is to help you learn and stay informed about issues and available solutions to getting better returns with low risk and cost.
