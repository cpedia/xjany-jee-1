Professional Content Management System

SiteMentrix is a professional online Content Management System (Wikipedia), also known as CMS. A CMS is a program or website that allows an individual to easily publish data, text, photos, audio, music and video to the Internet. It is software that allows you to easily make your own website, without having to learn the underlying technology such as HTML or programming. The user of the CMS just need to deal with the content of the website: so the text, images and sound. One of the features of the CMS is that it will display imported plain text in a clear and easy layout to your visitors. Almost all large companies and organizations use a CMS these days, but also many individuals, small businesses, foundations, associations and hobby clubs.
